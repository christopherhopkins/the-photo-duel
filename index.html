The Photo Duel

—

Two photographers spent one day shooting five themed rounds, side by side, same cameras, same clock. You don't know whose photo is whose — just Photo A vs Photo B. Score every image on the five marks, and the site tallies your verdict. Takes about five minutes.

how to score

The Five Marks — 1 (rough) to 5 (flawless)

Tap a photo to view it full-screen and flip between A and B. Then give each photo 1–5 on each mark. Go with your gut — the X-Factor is allowed to overrule everything.
